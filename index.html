Welcome to the Location-Service project

The project is dedicated for research purpose. If you want to be involved in the project, please contact the main developer. The contact information is located below this page.

Introduction

This Android app is exclusively used for research purpose. The project was created as a research for a class at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. The main use of this app is to track the location of the users in an interval of time.

Problems

Currently, the apps sample data every 10 minutes. This will kill the phone in less than 5 hours. One problem of the app, and for the majority of apps out there using sensors, is the energy spent. One major upgrade of this app is to come up with a energy saving algorithm for the Android platform.

Another problem is cycle of life of the Android app. Currently, Android OS will kill any non-important app if the memory of the phone is running low. This app is considered not important; therefore killing the app and stop sampling location data without informing the users.

New ideas are welcome!
